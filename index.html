GRAMMARPROF

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

GrammarProf is designed with privacy in mind. We collect only what is necessary to provide the service, we never sell your data, and you can delete everything at any time from the app.

01. Who We Are

GrammarProf ("the App", "we", "us") is an AI-powered grammar checker and writing assistant for mobile devices. The App is operated by an independent developer.

Contact: yaognalita@gmail.com

02. What Data We Collect

Account information

  • Email address (used to create your account and authenticate you)
  • Password (stored securely as a salted hash — we never see it in plain text)

Usage data

  • Text you submit for grammar checking or paraphrasing (processed to provide the service)
  • Your history of grammar checks and paraphrases (stored so you can review past results)
  • Daily usage counts (number of checks and paraphrases per day) to enforce your plan's limits

Purchase data

  • Your subscription plan (Free / Premium / More) — we store only the plan tier, not payment details. Payments are handled by Google Play and managed by RevenueCat.

Crash and error data

  • Crash reports and error logs (collected via Sentry) to help us fix bugs. These may include device type and OS version but are not linked to your identity.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, phone number, or location
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers
  • We do not store audio recordings from the voice input feature (audio is transcribed on-device or discarded immediately)

03. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the service — your text is sent to our AI processing server to generate corrections and paraphrases
  • To enforce usage limits — daily check counts are tracked server-side per account
  • To sync your history — if you are signed in, your history is stored in the cloud so you can access it across devices
  • To process payments — your subscription status is synced from RevenueCat to our database
  • To fix bugs — crash reports help us identify and resolve issues

04. AI Processing

Text you submit is processed through our secure server (a Supabase Edge Function) which calls the Anthropic Claude API. Your text is sent to Anthropic's servers to generate AI responses. Anthropic may process this data in accordance with their own privacy policy.

We do not use your text to train our models. We act as a proxy — your data is not stored by Anthropic beyond the processing of a single request.

For more information, see: Anthropic Privacy Policy

05. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Authentication and database hosting. Servers are located in the EU (eu-west region). Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic — AI text processing. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — In-app purchase management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Sentry — Crash reporting. Sentry Privacy Policy
  • Google Play — App distribution and payment processing. Google Privacy Policy

06. Data Retention

  • Your account and history data are retained as long as your account is active
  • Daily usage counts are reset each day
  • If you delete your account, all your data (account, history, usage records) is permanently deleted within 24 hours

07. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Access — you can request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — you can delete your account and all associated data directly from the app (Settings → Delete my account)
  • Portability — you can request your data in a portable format
  • Objection — you can object to certain processing activities

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at yaognalita@gmail.com.

08. Delete Your Account

You can delete your account and all your data directly from the app: Settings → Delete my account. This permanently removes your profile, history, and usage records. This action cannot be undone.

09. Children's Privacy

GrammarProf is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • Authentication is handled by Supabase with industry-standard JWT tokens
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text
  • Our AI proxy validates your identity before processing any request

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. For significant changes, we will notify you in the app. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at:
